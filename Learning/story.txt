A young boy named Arjun loved collecting old things. One Sunday, he visited a small market and found a dusty clock that didn't work.

"Why would you buy that?" his friend asked.

Arjun smiled. "Because everyone else thinks it's useless."

He bought the clock for ₹50 and took it home. When he opened the back cover, he discovered a folded piece of paper hidden inside. It was a note written 40 years ago:

The note was signed by a retired engineer named Mohan.

Inspired, Arjun started reading books about machines, electronics, and computers. Years later, he became an engineer and designed affordable solar-powered devices for villages.

One day, while giving a speech, he showed the old broken clock.

"This clock never worked," he said, "but it changed my life."

The audience laughed, and then applauded.
